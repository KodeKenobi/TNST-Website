About

Vuyo Wakaba is an experienced aviator who holds numerous academic and training qualifications including a Bachelor of Commerce degree in Strategic Management from Varsity College and a Post Graduate Diploma in Management Practice from Henley Business School. His organisational experience includes being a pilot for several organisations, including South African Airways, leading Flight Operations for a charter company and flight school and serving as the Deputy Manager for New Tempe Municipal Airport in Bloemfontein.

While serving as the Director for Operations and Organisational Effectiveness at The Now School of Thought, he is also pursuing a Master of Business Administration through Henley Business School. He is passionate about learning and skills development for the Fourth Industrial Revolution and seeks to convert the challenges being faced locally, regionally and internationally into opportunities for progress.

“We need to embrace the perspectives of others in order to better understand the challenges we must address and avoid the application of linear solutions to complex problems. TNST provides the vehicle to developing the attitude and mindset required for leadership and relevance in the Fourth Industrial Revolution”.
