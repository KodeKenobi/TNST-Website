About

Onkgopotse Matlala is the Director: Strategic Communications and Public Affairs at TNST. Her communications experience spans over 11 years. During this time, she obtained comprehensive knowledge in Internal and External Communications, Media Monitoring, policy-making and brand and events management.

Onkgopotse is committed to leveraging the skills she has acquired to foster and maintain positive relationships between TNST and its stakeholders, and support the organisation’s mandate in preparing the next generation of leaders for the opportunities and challenges that await them.

“Too often, we give children answers to remember than problems to solve”- Roger Lewin.

Onkgopotse believes that is it time that learners are also inspired on how to think and not what to think, an invaluable skill required to succeed in life. The programmes offered by TNST aim to fill this gap to aid learners in transitioning from high school to university, the labour market and in navigating life in general.

Onkgopotse holds a National Diploma in International Communications from the Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) and a Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of South Africa (UNISA).
