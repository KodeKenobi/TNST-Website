About

Khanyi Masuku is TNST’s Lead for communications planning and execution, and not just because she has a warm smile and a sharp mind. Ms Masuku initially joined TNST as a Researcher, and as the operating model became robust, TNST balanced certainties with odds and realised that the future value Ms Masuku would bring to the organisation could be greater if her potential was to be recognised Now!

“TNST has embodied shared-value in its personality. The possibilities for involvement with TNST are immense and productivity and delivery have been designed to not only serve but to also build the people”.

Ms Masuku believes that embracing the experience economy is crucial because TNST delegates and partner networks are value-conscious and are driven by their experience with our engagements. Khanyi is a senior student at Vega and is enrolled for a BA Strategic Brand Communication degree.
