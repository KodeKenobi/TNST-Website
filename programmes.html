Our Programmes

Our programme mix is focused on designing transformative learning experiences with facilitating conditions for our delegates to assume a position of positive impact in society. TNST relies on reach and impact as key drivers in inspiring desirable school-leaver attributes such as critical thinking skills and personal mastery, for positive learner outcomes such as adaptability and resourcefulness post basic education.

TNST Master Classes

TNST Master Classes are nation-wide evening programmes which TNST delivers anywhere in the country made possible by donor patronage and partners. In light of the digital divide in regions where meaningful development is needed, TNST facilitators will travel to locations where the programme is needed despite being a virtual school. TNST Enlightenment Programme

The Enlightenment Programme has a unique architecture of topical discussions which places our delegates in reflective positions of themselves using personal mastery constructs, critical thinking and reasoning, as well as thought leadership. The more our delegates know, is the better they can transition to any pathway post basic education engagements. The programme runs 1 workshop every week per cohort in keeping with a particular theme for the month. Each week in the month presents a discussion topic as composite towards the desired outcomes of the theme and the perceived social fitness of the topics
